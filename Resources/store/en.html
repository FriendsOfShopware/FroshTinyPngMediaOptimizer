Optimize your images as best as possible

Stop torturing your visitors with long loading times!

TinyPNG.com is the best

This plugin optimizes your PNGs from the shop using TinyPNG.com.
All new images are automatically optimized.
From our own experience, TinyPNG.com is the best image optimizer for JPGs - even for our friend Google.

No JPGs?

We deliberately avoided implementing PNGs because TinyPNG.com can not compress them optimally.
For the optimization of JPGs, we clearly recommend optimus.io (Not in the Community Store yet).

"I already have images in shop!"

No problem! There are several ways to optimize your existing images:

  1. Generate via Command alle Thumbnails neu: php bin/console sw:thumbnail:generate -f
  2. Via Backend in the media manager using "Generate Thumbnauls" in albums settings. The batch size should be "1"!

Recommendations

Additional thumbnail sizes when using the responsive theme:

Article: 50x50, 86x86, 170x170, 430x430
Shopping worlds: 293x293, 931x931, 1160x1160
